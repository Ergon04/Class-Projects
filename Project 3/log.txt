You will create a simple banking system that handles various account operations while gracefully managing errors and unusual circumstances. This assignment emphasizes making your program robust through proper exception handling.

Learning Outcomes
Enhance Program Robustness: Learn how to improve the robustness of a program by designing it to handle unusual circumstances and erroneous input gracefully, ensuring it does not crash during unexpected scenarios.
Understand Exceptions: Deepen your understanding of the difference between checked and unchecked exceptions, and gain practical experience in both throwing and catching exceptions of each kind.
Work with ArrayList: Gain additional practice using ArrayList in Java.
Test Exception Handling: Strengthen your testing skills by writing tests that specifically verify whether exceptions are thrown under prescribed circumstances and ensure they are handled appropriately
